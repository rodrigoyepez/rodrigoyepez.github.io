## Overview

Architected and maintained a 10TB SQL database to support AI cancer classification research. This project involved collaborating with cross-disciplinary teams to standardize diverse data formats and ensure consistent database integration.

## Technical Challenges

- **Scale**: Managing 10+ terabytes of medical imaging and clinical data
- **Data Standardization**: Converting diverse data formats and units into consistent schemas
- **Integration**: Aligning technical solutions with research and clinical needs
- **Compliance**: Ensuring data accessibility while maintaining research compliance

## Key Contributions

- Designed efficient database schemas for heterogeneous medical data
- Built ETL pipelines for continuous data ingestion
- Created documentation ensuring reproducibility across research workflows
- Collaborated weekly with cross-disciplinary teams to align technical solutions

## Technologies

`SQL` `Database Design` `ETL Pipelines` `Data Engineering` `Python` `Linux`
